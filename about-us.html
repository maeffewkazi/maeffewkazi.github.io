Our Mission

We believe in finding practical solutions that support an efficient, safe and sustainable future for the lives we touch every day. This is why we work in close collaboration with our clients turning great ideas into tangible reality as we strive to Make Everyday Possible™.

make everyday

possible

Spline Group is a multi-disciplinary engineering firm providing services to the construction industry. Backed by a team of professional consulting engineers, project management experts, LEED accredited professionals, and designers, the firm offers a broad range of engineering experience and has emerged as a leading firm. Involvement in numerous projects throughout Canada and around the world has resulted in solid relationships with a broad range of clients in both the private and public sectors.

Relentless Communication

At Spline, we’re committed to ensuring the success of every project, and we champion all projects through effective communication. Our process is simple — we start by recording the project direction, followed by communicating with stakeholders, project coordinators and consultants, carrying this information through each subsequent phase of design and construction.

We provide the experience and expertise needed to deliver successful project outcomes for our clients and add value to their businesses.
