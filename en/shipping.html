Shipping

Standard delivery service

The standard delivery service is done by truck by delivery experts. Your package will be carefully wrapped and delivered to your home. This service is best suited for small furniture pieces such as the Halo 01 collection.

White gloves delivery service

For bigger and heavier pieces such as the Halo 02 and Halo 03 collections, opt for white gloves delivery service. Experienced delivery experts will carefully uncrate the furniture, bring it in your home and place it in the room of your choice. No packaging will be left on site.

For additional information concerning maintenance, please contact us at infos@loevenmorcel.com.
