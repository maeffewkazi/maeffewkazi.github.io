Customizable dimensions

The standard dimensions of the OH collection do not suit you? Please note that all of our furniture can be modified. Contact us for more details.

White gloves delivery service

For bigger and heavier pieces such as the Halo 02 and Halo 03 collections, opt for white gloves delivery service. Experienced delivery experts will carefully uncrate the furniture, bring it in your home and place it in the room of your choice. No packaging will be left on site.

green packaging

In the custom manufacturing world, it is sometimes difficult to maximize the cuts and not to generate material losses.

In the perspective of wanting to reduce our ecological footprint, we have decided to design our own packaging for delivery from our cut scraps!
