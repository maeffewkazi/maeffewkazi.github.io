Sr. Mechanical Engineer

We are looking for a senior Mechanical Engineer with 7 to 10 years of experience. Revit experience a must! The Senior Mechanical Designer is responsible for the design of mechanical systems including HVAC, plumbing, drainage and fire protection and lead / manages a team of Mechanical designers, technicians and CAD drafters.
Qualifications
  • At least 7-10 years of experience in the Engineering Consulting industry
  • Registration as a Professional Engineer in Ontario (P.ENG)
  • Experience with reading and coordinating Mechanical, Electrical, Structural and Architectural drawings
  • Familiar with OBC, NFPA, ASHRAE and other relevant Codes and Standards
  • Strong working knowledge of AutoCAD and REVIT
