Creative Campus

If we want to see more diversity in creative departments, we need to look beyond current talent pools and address the fundamental challenge – the pipeline.

Talent from underrepresented backgrounds are often unaware of the wide variety of creative jobs that are out there – and how to pursue them.

Creative Campus equips the world’s most promising, diverse up-and-comers with deeper knowledge of the industry, a wider understanding of their career potential, as well as the tools, mentors, and confidence they need to embark on their careers.

Applications for 2020 intake of Creative Campus have now closed.
