Completed

The approach

Our clients had newly purchased this residential property and were looking for help to make the best use of the compact ground floor plan.

MLA opted to remove the internal walls to the rear of the property in order to create an open plan family living area. The clients wanted to keep the property’s period features, so any new alterations/additions were sensitively considered. A simple material palette was selected, with off tone colours contrasting against the white backdrop.

A small and simple renovation completed for our clients which successfully executed the brief within budget.
